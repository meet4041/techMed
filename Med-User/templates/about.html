About Us

Our Story

The story begins from a busy medical shop of a particular area which helps the team leader to think of an idea. That's how techMed was born. It's an easy, user friendly and one step solution to place an order and collect the order at out own desired time without waiting in a queue or wasting any of your precious time.


Our Mission

As people hesitate to wait in a queue for more than 10-15 minutes and later it may also found that the prescribed medicine by the doctor is not available or out of stock at that chemist shop , so here techMed came in action and you can order before leaving the house/office and once the order get ready you are informed by call or text and you can collect your order without wasting your time. This is the main moto of techMed.


Our Goal

Our mission is not only to connect the user with only one pharmacy shop , we dream to become zomato in pharmacy, in which we will try to connect each and every chemist shop of user's near by area and later on an option of delivery will also be introduced to ease the work for the user.
